ABOUT

Hi, I’m Joe - the nose behind Scooshy.

I adore fragrances.

I’m the guy who always awkwardly asks the other guy what he’s wearing.

A self-proclaimed connoisseur, I have a profound love for sniffing out new scents.

Driven by my passion for both fragrances and writing, I decided to create Scooshy, with a little (a lot of) help from the fantastic designers who work alongside me in my real job as Marketing Director at BrightByte Studio. A special thanks to Mitch, Andre and Matt for making this possible.

Scooshy is just a creative way of sharing my fragrance reviews with like-minded fragrance advocates or anyone who's willing to read them.

Other sensory delights include coffee (Guatemalan my current favourite), wine of many great varieties, lots of cheese, cycling, rock-climbing and my awesome girlfriend.

Everyone is welcome at Scooshy, your opinions are actively encouraged and greatly valued. Well, almost everyone; spammers please go and do the world a favour...

Thanks for stopping by, enjoy!

Joe Perkins – Founder at Scooshy.
